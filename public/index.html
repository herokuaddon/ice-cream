Beginner Recipe

Simple Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk milk, sugar, vanilla, and salt until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Stir in heavy cream until smooth.
  3. Chill the mixture for at least 2 hours.
  4. Churn in an ice cream maker according to machine instructions.
  5. Transfer to a freezer-safe container.
  6. Freeze until firm, then serve with your favorite toppings.

Dessert Advice

Helpful Ice Cream Tips

Chill the Mixture

Cold ice cream mixture churns better and can create a smoother texture. Chill your base before freezing or churning.

Store Properly

Keep ice cream in an airtight freezer-safe container to reduce ice crystals and help preserve flavor.

Add Toppings Before Serving

Add sauces, fruit, nuts, cookies, or sprinkles right before serving for better texture and freshness.
